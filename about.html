About Us

Asahina Sushi, dedicated to creating a truly unique and luxurious dining experience. Asahina Sushi is renowned for its exceptional expertise in the art of Omasake-style sushi and we are commitment to providing the utmost satisfaction to our customers.Asahina Sushi, based in Ottawa city, embarked on its culinary journey in 2022, fueled by a deep passion for showcasing the authentic essence of Japanese cuisine. The visionary founders identified a market gap in Ottawa for a sushi restaurant that not only served mouthwatering sushi but also placed a strong emphasis on harmonizing it with perfectly paired sake. With this vision as their guiding force, they dedicated themselves to establishing an extraordinary dining establishment that would redefine the sushi experience in Ottawa and elevate it to unparalleled heights

Our Vision

A Vision Transformed into Every Dish, uncover the Secrets of Authentic Japanese Cuisine at Asahina Sushi

Our company vision is to redefine the sushi experience with unmatched luxury, culinary expertise, and a commitment to customer satisfaction. Indulge in Omasake-style sushi and sake pairing, embracing authentic flavors and traditions of Japan.
